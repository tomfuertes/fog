What you get

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Six first-principles features, no complexity traps.

🔒

GDPR Compliant by Design

HMAC identity with daily salt rotation. No cookies, no localStorage, no fingerprinting. CNIL-approved pattern - no consent banner required.

📊

Bayesian Statistics

Clear "probability B beats A" instead of confusing p-values. Auto-stops experiments when significance is reached.

🚩

Feature Flags

Ramp-stable progressive rollout. Visitors ON at 10% stay ON at 50%. isEnabled() one-liner API.

💰

Auto Revenue Tracking

Intercepts GA4 dataLayer purchase events automatically. Zero manual instrumentation needed for e-commerce experiments.

⚡

~2KB SDK

ESM + IIFE. One script tag for analytics, npm package for experiments. No build step required for the drop-in snippet.

🏠

Self-Hosted

Runs on your Cloudflare Workers account. Free tier handles most sites. Your data, your infrastructure, your rules.
